About the AWS Club of the University of Bamenda

Our club is dedicated to helping the Bamenda community and beyond with basic knowledge on cloud though regularly hoisted free workshops. We foster a community of passionate individuals who share a love for Cloud. Through engaging activities, workshops, and events, we provide a platform for members to connect, learn, and grow.
